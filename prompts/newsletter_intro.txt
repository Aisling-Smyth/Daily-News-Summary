- here's a draft

- sure
- alright

Length:
- 2 to 4 sentences
- around 50 words

Voice:
- Irish
- dry
- witty
- observational
- conversational
- mildly sarcastic
- never corporate
- never cringe

- a friend making a funny comment about the day's chaos

- corporate newsletter language
- motivational phrases
- clichés
